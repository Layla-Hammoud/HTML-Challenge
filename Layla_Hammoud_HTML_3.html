Where is Codi located?

Codi operates in two locations in Lebanon; office hours are 9:00 to 5:00 pm, Monday to Friday.

What programmes does Codi offer in Lebanon?

Currently, we offer our Core (Full-Stack Web Development Programme) in Beirut and Tripoli. We also run community-based workshops yearly (please check our social media for more information).

Who is Codi?

Codi is a leading non-governmental organisation (NGO) in Lebanon, operating in 2017, that provides accessible and transformative education driving economic growth and social change. Open and accessible to all, our Core programme combines a coding boot camp and a leadership school, and it is designed and continuously improved to cultivate lifelong learners and Full-Stack Web Developers (89% employment rate).

Is the Core online or in-person?

The Core programme follows a hybrid learning model, with most face-to-face (in-person) learning. Students will be working 1 day remotely on a weekly basis after Prairie. Prairie refers to the first 6 weeks of the Core programme, which is the final selection phase of the programme.
